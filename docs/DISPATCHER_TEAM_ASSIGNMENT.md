# Dispatcher Team Assignment - Clarification

## Question
Are dispatchers supposed to be part of teams?

## Answer
**YES** - Dispatchers are part of the **Logistics & Warehouse** team.

## Dispatcher's Role in the Team

The dispatcher serves as the **coordination hub** for the warehouse team:

### Primary Responsibilities:
- **Job Assignment**: Assigns pick, pack, and putaway jobs to team members
- **Workflow Coordination**: Ensures smooth flow from receiving → putaway → picking → packing → dispatch
- **Resource Management**: Balances workload across pickers and other warehouse staff
- **Communication**: Acts as liaison between warehouse operations and delivery/transport

### Team Interactions:
- **Reports to**: Warehouse Manager
- **Assigns work to**: Pickers, Packers
- **Coordinates with**: Drivers, Inventory Specialists
- **Supports**: Overall warehouse efficiency and throughput

## System Permissions

Dispatchers have access to:
- ✅ WMS Dashboard (with all KPIs after recent fix)
- ✅ PICK tab (assign and monitor picking jobs)
- ✅ PACK tab (coordinate packing operations)
- ✅ PUTAWAY tab (manage putaway jobs)
- ✅ DISPATCH tab (coordinate outbound shipments)
- ✅ RECEIVE tab (oversee receiving operations)
- ✅ RETURNS tab (manage return processing)

## Conclusion

Dispatchers are **essential team members** of the Logistics & Warehouse department. They are not solo operators but rather the coordination layer that ensures the warehouse team works efficiently as a cohesive unit.
